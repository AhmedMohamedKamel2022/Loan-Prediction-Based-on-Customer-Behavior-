About

Did you know that machine learning models can help you predict many things with great accuracy?!😲
The organization wants to anticipate potential defaulters for a consumer loan product. They have data about historical customer behavior based on what they have observed. Hence, when they acquire new clients, they want to predict who is riskier and who isn't. Therefore, this application is designed to help identify the behavior of new customers.

To see the behavior of new customers, simply follow the steps bellow:
- Filling the data that describe him ✍.
- Press "Predict" and wait for the result 💗.
